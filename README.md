# AI BRD Generator (GenAI Business Analyst Project)

## Problem
- BRDs are created using stakeholders’ inputs, meeting notes, and emails
- Consolidating information is time-consuming and inconsistent
- Poorly written BRDs can lead to rework, delayed projects, and scope issues

## Business Users
The most common users who use insights to convert it into BRDs are: 
- Business Analyst 
- Product Managers
- IT Consultants 
- Project Managers

## Current Challenges
- Requirements are scattered across emails, various documents, and meeting notes
- Analysts struggle to structure BRDs correctly
- Time spent rewriting and validating requirements is high
- Inconsistent formats across teams

## Proposed AI Solution
- An AI powered BRD Generator that uses Generative AI to automatically convert unstructured business inputs into a structured, standardized BRD

## Inputs
- Stakeholders’ emails, meeting notes, a brief business problem description, optional templates, or company standards

## AI Logic
- AI model analyzes unstructured text
- Extracts key business requirements, assumptions, and constraints
- Identifies missing or unclear requirements
- Structures content into predefined BRD sections

## Outputs
Complete BRD including: 
- business objectives
- functional requirements
- non-functional requirements
- assumptions, and risks

## Business Value
- Reduces BRD creation time by 50%
- Improves the quality of requirements
- Improves consistency
- Enables analysts to work more effectively
- Reduces rework and miscommunication between business and IT teams

## Success Metrics
- Time taken to create BRD
- Number of requirement revisions
- Stakeholder satisfaction score
